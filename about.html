About Us

Who Are We?

UVPF start up labs is the wing of Udyam Vidya Prasar Foundation, structured as section 8 company, working with academic institutions and universities for creating and nurturing innovation and startup ecosystem in their campus. It is an association of professionals working in skill development, entrepreneurship, innovation, and incubation since last 17 years.

Our goal is to create a vibrant and sustainable start-up ecosystem within academic institutions that lead to technology commercialization, innovation utilization and wealth generation for the nation.

UVPF Start Up lab also nurtures and mentors the local production units and markets to expand their business. We also work to generate business opportunities and develop ecosystem in underprivileged regions ensuring sustainable growth of the region
