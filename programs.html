Programs We Offer

Programs for Startups

Market Validation and B-Plan Preparation Support Program: We help you validate your idea and prepare a solid business plan.

Virtual Incubation Program: Join our virtual incubation program and get all kinds of support for your startup.

Prototype Development Support Program: We plan, design, and develop the prototype and MVP of your product or service idea.

University & Institutes Programs

Yearlong Program for Innovation and Startup Development: We set up our “UVPF Start Up Support Center” in the campus of universities or institutes and execute this structured program.

Association for Startup Development, Mentoring, and Co-working: Through this association, we work with startups as mentors or co-founders and ensure their success.

Association for Educational Programs: We conduct certificate courses, minor degree courses, short-term training, workshops, boot camps, and awareness programs on innovation, entrepreneurship, and startups.

UVP Foundation

Udyam Vidya Prasar Foundation is a section 8 company, working in startup and entrepreneurship, skill development, education, research and development, innovation commercialization, and self-employment. It is an association of professionals who are working in skill development, innovation, incubation, and entrepreneurship for the last 17 years.
